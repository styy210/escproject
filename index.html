About Us

Get involved with us

At ESC, we are dedicated to the school group project focused on e waste collection, management, and recycling. Our mission is to motivate others to refrain from discarding electronic waste irresponsibly and instead, entrust it to us for proper handling and recycling. With a strong commitment to sustainability and environmental consciousness, our team of students collaborates passionately with the community to raise awareness and drive positive change. From the initial collection to the recycling process, we prioritize efficient management and innovative solutions, ensuring that every electronic item is handled responsibly. Whether you have old gadgets to dispose of, unused electronics cluttering your space, or simply want to contribute to a greener future, we are here to assist. Witness the impact of responsible e-waste management and recycling with our ESC Team.

Our Project

Eco-friendly collection

We aim disposal of electronic waste in an environmentally conscious manner. Regardless of the size or type of electronic device, our team ensures proper disposal and recycling.

Sustainable management

We are committed to sustainable management of e-waste by ensuring efficient recycling and responsible disposal. We focus on reducing, reusing, and recycling electronic products to minimize environmental impact and conserve resources.

Recycling initiatives

Our recycling initiative focuses on creating accessible collection points and promoting community participation in e-waste recycling. We partner with certified recycling centers to ensure that discarded electronics are processed responsibly, aiming to reduce waste and recover valuable materials.
